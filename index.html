HONEY DAO 

Who are we?
HoneyDAO is a collective of DeFi influencers, seasoned investors, crypto developers and founders who have joined forces to provide value in the form of capital investment as well as social, community, development and commercial support. HoneyDAO is unique in its approach by allowing all members regardless of size to access allocation while giving an equal vote to each member, representing a truly fair yet powerful approach we call the hive-effect.

We emerged in April 2021 with a fair-launch distribution, quickly boasting 100 genesis members. The DAO will soon open up again to more member-bees with the goal of being the number one fair-venture-DAO in DeFi. 

What do we do?
HoneyDAO has access to large amounts of pooled capital, skills and influence. Early-stage (or later-stage) DeFi projects can apply for support from HoneyDAO which will then be assessed, if needed adjusted and finally put to a vote. HoneyDAO does more than just capital injections, it provides social support, marketing support, commercial support, development and skill support and represents an instant pool of users for supported partners.

How can you get involved?
If you are an investor, you can join our Discord group for more information on how to apply as a member of the DAO. If you are a promising DeFi project looking for seed-round value, you can follow the instructions in our documents on how to begin the application process. Otherwise, interested projects can reach out to a DAO member in our Discord or send an email to the DAO.
